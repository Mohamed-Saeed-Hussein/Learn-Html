AUGUST 2025: WE ARE GOING SEED OIL FREE!

Beginning August 2025, we are switching our primary cooking oils to Zero Acre Farms Cultured Sugarcane Oil & Fera Fruit Oil. The only other cooking fats we use in our kitchen are Straus Organic Butter, house rendered duck fat, and extra virgin olive oil. We are no longer using any industrially produced seed oils in our kitchen.

How to Reserve a Table

The table reservation process typically involves several steps, from the initial request to the confirmation of the reservation:

Step 1: Initial Request

Provide details like date, time, number of guests, and any special requests. The restaurant checks availability and responds.

Step 2: Availability Check

The restaurant checks seating capacity and any overlapping events or reservations.

Step 3: Reservation Confirmation

If available, the restaurant confirms the reservation (via email, phone, or in person) and provides a confirmation code.

Step 4: Reservation Management

The restaurant updates seating charts, notifies staff, and prepares for the reservation.
